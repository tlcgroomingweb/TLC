A clearer version of TLC's appointment policies

Each time slot is reserved for one pet, and TLC's groomers work on commission. This keeps the policy visible without turning it into a heavy legal page.

Back to booking Call if running late
Running late Call before the appointment time

If you are late for drop-off or pick-up, call TLC. Late drop-offs may need to be cancelled or rescheduled.

Late pick-up 30-minute pickup window

After the 30-minute window, a $15 fee applies for every 15 minutes late.

New clients No-show steps for first-time appointments

The first missed appointment requires a deposit before rebooking. Continued no-shows become stricter.

1st no-show 50% deposit before the next appointment.
2nd no-show 50% paid upfront to book again.
3rd no-show Future appointments are no longer available.
Regular clients No-show steps for returning clients

Regular clients get a lighter first reminder, then deposit requirements if missed appointments continue.

1st no-show $20 added to the next appointment.
2nd no-show 50% deposit required to book again.
3rd no-show Future appointments are no longer permitted.
